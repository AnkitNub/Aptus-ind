About Aptus Industries

Aptus proudly supplies a superior range of rice varieties, carefully sourced and processed to ensure the highest levels of purity, taste, and nutrition. Our diverse portfolio includes premium basmati and non-basmati rice, organic and specialty rice, parboiled and raw rice, and customized packaging solutions. These products are crafted to deliver exceptional quality, cost-effectiveness, and reliability, catering to a wide array of consumer and industrial needs. With a strong focus on customer satisfaction and a commitment to excellence, we bring forward solutions that empower businesses and households to enjoy nutritious and flavorful rice.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision is to bring the essence of purity and tradition to every home with the finest grains of rice—nurturing trust, quality, and well-being in every meal.


We are on a mission to deliver more than just rice—we bring authenticity, quality, and trust to every grain. By partnering with passionate farmers and upholding the highest standards, we ensure that every meal made with our rice is filled with purity and nourishment. Committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing, we strive to create lasting relationships with our customers, making every bite a testament to our dedication and care.
